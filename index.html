Services

Our Services

Weekend Temple Trips

Experience divine weekend journeys like the Kolluru Mookambika Temple and Mangaluru (Daiva Nadu) tours. Includes A/C travel, meals, accommodation, and guided sightseeing.

Hotel & Stay Arrangements

Comfortable and affordable accommodation is included in every package. We ensure a clean, safe, and relaxing stay for all our travelers.

Malayali Tour Guides

Our friendly and experienced Malayali guides accompany you throughout the trip, ensuring a smooth, enjoyable, and culturally rich travel experience.

Customized Group Tours

Plan special group trips, family pilgrimages, or corporate travel packages tailored to your schedule and preferences.

Comfortable A/C Transfers

Enjoy your journey in well-maintained A/C tempo travellers and cars with professional drivers ensuring safety and comfort throughout your trip.

Meals & Refreshments

Every package includes delicious breakfast, lunch, dinner, and tea snacks — freshly prepared and suited for your travel schedule.

Sightseeing & Activities

From temple visits to beachside camel rides, every trip is designed to give you a perfect mix of spirituality, fun, and relaxation.

Special Festival Packages

Celebrate festivals like Diwali with our exclusive getaway tours such as the Mangaluru Daiva Nadu trip — a spiritual and refreshing experience.
